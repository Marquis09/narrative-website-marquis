Traditional attire at oktoberfest.

Men: Suspenders are worn with leatherhosen (leather shorts). Checkered or white shirts are preferred. turdy shoes and knee-length socks are recommended.

Women: Dirndls with blouses are traditional Bavarian clothing. The waist is tied with an apron.

Accessories for Both: It is optional to wear a hat, traditional jewelry, and a traditional jacket. There is no strict dress code for Oktoberfest, but attendees wear a variety of styles, including traditional attire.

Munich's Oktoberfest mainly offers classic Bavarian brews that follow the German Beer Purity Law (Reinheitsgebot). Among the primary kinds are:

Märzen:

A classic German beer connected to Oktoberfest is called Märzen. It is golden in colour, tastes malty with hints of caramel and toast, and has a moderate 5.5% to 6.3% ABV alcohol level. It's a mainstay of Oktoberfest's celebratory beer lineup.

Weißbier (Wheat Beer):

German wheat beer known as Weißbier is frequently served in tall glasses and is distinguished by its fruity flavour with notes of clove and banana, as well as its light and hazy appearance. A cool option for Oktoberfest and beyond.

Helles:

Helles is a light-colored German beer with a clean, mildly malty flavour and a modest 4.5% to 5.5% ABV alcohol content. It's widely loved, especially during Oktoberfest, and makes a refreshing contrast to darker beers.

The Oktoberfest Parade:

The Oktoberfest Parade in Munich, Germany.

Munich hosts a vibrant parade known as the Oktoberfest, complete with horse-drawn beer waggons, brass bands and traditional costumes.

More about the parade:

As the festival makes its way to the Theresienwiese fairgrounds, it opens with a display of Bavarian culture. With its vibrant decorations and period costumes, the procession creates a joyous atmosphere.

A simple oktoberfest meal:

Grilled to perfection, Oktoberfest sausages (Bratwurst and Weisswurst) are served with sauerkraut and mustard. A tasty and traditional food at the festival, the doughy, huge soft pretzels go great with cheese dip or mustard.
